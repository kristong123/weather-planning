Proposal: Weather and Planning App

Problem Overview

Planning outdoor activities around the weather is a common challenge, especially for people with busy schedules. The unpredictability of weather conditions often forces last-minute changes to plans or missed opportunities for exciting activities. However, most calendar and weather apps exist individually, leaving users to manually align the two. This disconnect becomes more frustrating as the weather fluctuates and people's schedules change back and forth. A key information technology problem is the lack of intelligent integration between weather forecasting services and personal scheduling tools. Existing solutions, like weather apps (OpenWeatherMap) or digital calendars (Google Calendar), do not automatically suggest or reschedule activities based on forecasted conditions. Although some apps have attempted to bridge this gap, they often lack dynamic calendar syncing or deep personalization. They fail to consider calendar availability or allow for detailed user preferences tied to specific weather types. As a result, there remains a need for a system that not only understands user preferences and schedules but also makes proactive, weather-smart planning decisions.

App Description

Our app is intended for users of all ages and backgrounds who want to schedule activities based on their personal schedules and preferred weather conditions. It will be particularly helpful for users who wish to do activities that require specific weather conditions, such as skiing or snowboarding, surfing, or cycling.

Users can interact with the app by inputting their regular schedule into the built-in calendar feature. Events such as classes or work will be included here. Then, the user can input extra activities they want to do into another section. Here, they can specify the weather conditions they prefer for these activities. For example, if they wanted to go skiing, it would be preferable if it snowed the day or night before. Then, at the click of a button, the app will connect with a weather API called Open-metro Free Weather API and automatically generate a schedule that incorporates these additional activities into the user's calendar at specific dates and times that match the preferred weather conditions for each activity. If the user would like to make changes to the schedule given to them, they can modify the schedule and move items around to their liking.

This app solves the problem of planning extracurricular activities around normally busy schedules. Instead of manually comparing weather and scheduling apps, ours combines both functionalities. This saves time and incentivizes people to be more proactive and flexible in planning.
